About Dallas College

Dallas College is a premier community college serving the Dallas County area with seven campuses across the metroplex. Founded in 1965, we have grown to become one of the largest community college systems in Texas.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable education that prepares students for successful careers and transfer to four-year institutions. We serve over 80,000 students annually through credit and continuing education programs.

Dallas College is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC) to award associate degrees and certificates.

Admissions Process

Applying to Dallas College is simple and straightforward. We have an open admissions policy for most programs, welcoming all students who are ready to pursue higher education.

Submit your free online application through our website. You'll need to provide basic personal information and your educational background.

Provide official high school transcripts or GED scores. If you have previous college credits, submit those transcripts for evaluation.

Complete the FAFSA to determine your eligibility for grants, loans, and work-study programs. Dallas College's school code is 016792.

Meet with an academic advisor to select your courses and register for classes. New student orientation is available to help you get started.
